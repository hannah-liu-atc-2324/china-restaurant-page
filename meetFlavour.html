Meet Flavour!

OUR FAMILY
We are known for cooking specialty Xinjiang dishes.Xinjiang cuisine is predominantly Halal, mostly eating beef and mutton, with famous delicacies such as roasted whole goat, big plate chicken, hand-held rice, and stewed mutton.
OUR FOOD
Flavour Restaurant has a wide range of specialties and a cozy environment. The dishes are all derived from cuisines, thus attracting diners of all needs.
OUR CHEFS
Our professional chefs have 20 years of experience cooking Uyghur cuisine in the Xinjiang region and also consistently focus on and innovate our dishes.
